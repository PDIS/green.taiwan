The Action Plan will combine resources from academic institutions, research institutes, state-owned businesses and the industries. The precursor policies are Two-year Plan for Solar Energy, Countrywide Green Energy Rooftops, Four-year Plan for Wind Energy, and Green Energy Technology Forward-thinking Infrastructure. The Action Plan aims to boost employment through inducing domestic demand, and at the same time, the green innovation industrial ecosystem behind the conception of Shalun Smart Green Energy Science City, seeks to raise Taiwan's competitiveness in the green energy industry, such that we may stand shoulder to shoulder with the best in the international markets.
public constructions required for the R&D and industry development of green technology, including reinforcing solar PV R&D, and the construction of underwater foundations and wharfs for wind farms.
Build "Shalun Smart Green Energy Science City" on the border of Kaohsiung and Tainan, and establish a joint research center to further foster technologies of Technology Readiness Level(TRL) 4-6. There will be demonstration fields for technologies of TRL 6-8. Together with good quality of living environment, capital investments and other services, this Platform will ameliorate the overall environment of green energy technology development while facilitating the commercialization of R&D results.
Create green business opportunities through the green energy technology commercialization verification platform, regional energy storage technology demo and verification, and third-party verification center for renewable energy investment and financing. It will create a win-win situation for both corporations and financial institutions, accelerating the deployment of renewable energy nationwide.
