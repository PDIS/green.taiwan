For anyone in the manufacturing and service industries, research institutes, general public and individual inventors, the website provides the following services: information disclosure of corporate rewards and assistances, online applications and progress inquiries, governmental cross-agency verification and audits, MIT Smile logo verification, and project inquiries and consultation pertaining to intellectual property in a section dedicated to the patent commercialization.
